FAQ

Everything travelers ask before booking.

Do you book my hotel or Airbnb?

No. MY WORLD GURU does not arrange, recommend or book accommodations. You handle lodging independently — we coordinate everything else around it: transfers, daily plans and pick-ups.

What does the Silver / Gold fee actually cover?

The fee covers your Guru's consulting time — the intake review, the video call, building the custom itinerary, securing restaurant reservations and (on Gold) the on-island arrival consultation. Excursion and transfer costs are separate.

Which messaging apps do you support?

WhatsApp, WeChat, LINE, Viber, FB Messenger, Telegram and iMessage. Pick whichever you use most — your Guru is on it.

What if the weather changes my plans?

That's exactly why Gold tier exists. Your Guru re-sequences your day in real time — swapping a cruise for a winery, a hike for a beach club — based on the live forecast.

Can I bring my own wishlist of restaurants & tours?

Absolutely encouraged. Drop the names you found on Instagram, TikTok or travel blogs into the form — we'll secure the reservations and integrate them into your itinerary.

Is there a cruise-ship day package?

Yes. Tell us your docking hours and we'll design an hour-by-hour plan that beats the caldera peak crowds and gets you back on board on time, guaranteed.
